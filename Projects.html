Brick Breaker

Brick Breaker is a timeless arcade classic that captivates players with its addictive gameplay and retro charm. In this iconic game, players control a paddle at the bottom of the screen, tasked with deflecting a bouncing ball to destroy a wall of colorful bricks above. With each successful hit, bricks disintegrate into satisfying bursts of pixels, while power-ups and bonuses enhance the gameplay experience. As players progress through increasingly challenging levels, they must master precision and timing to clear the screen and advance to new stages.
